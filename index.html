About This Project

Nescot.uk is a student-driven initiative designed to offer an alternative perspective on Nescot College. Created by creative media students, this website serves as a hub for insights, updates, and resources for anyone interested in learning more about the college from a student viewpoint.

LRC Toilets Temporarily Out of Order

November 28, 2024

The LRC Toilets are currently out of order. Here are the alternative toilets you can use:

  • Annexe near the Nurse's Office: Available after 3PM.
  • Second Floor Near Security: If it is before 3PM, head up the staircase near security on the second floor, turn right, and the toilets will be the first on your left. (Note: Not accessible if you are unable to walk upstairs.)
  • Ground Floor for Central and North Wings: Use the lift near the central and north wings. Exit the lift and turn left. Head straight until you see a doorway on the left. Walk through the doors and go diagonally to the doors ahead of you. The toilets will be on your left.
  • The Yukon: Find the entrance facing the turnstile exit alleyway. When inside, make a right and continue straight. The toilets will be on the left.
  • B2B Block: The B2B Block is the big gray building located between the LRC and Yukon buildings. Enter through the two sets of doors into the lobby area. Find the doors on the right side (from the perspective of entering the building). The toilets are located to the left and right of the second set of doors.

There is no estimated time for the LRC Toilets to reopen, but they are expected to be operational again soon.
